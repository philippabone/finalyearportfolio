Susan's Farmhouse Fudge

ABOUT US

In 1983 Susan began producing fudge in the family kitchen on the banks on the River Ribble.
To this day it contains no preservatives or gluten and is made with only the best ingredients - farm fresh cream, real butter and british sugar
