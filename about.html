About Us

How it all started ...

In 1983 Susan began producing fudge in the family kitchen on the banks on the river Ribble, using a recipe that has been handed through the generations for more than 100 years.

This delicious fudge has been well known throughout the country for over 30 years and we can be found in many a field at events.

To this day it contains no preservatives or gluten and is made with only the best ingredients - farm fresh cream, real butter and british sugar. All our products are produced in small quantities on the stove to ensure the traditional crumbly home-made fudge that is well loved. Â 

A taste of the good old days - come and taste the difference.
